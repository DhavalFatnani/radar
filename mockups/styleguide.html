The visual language · UIUX spec §2–§3

A calm, confident intelligence workspace.

Not a consumer app — a precision instrument for one operator, all day. Color is spent almost entirely on meaning; data is monospace; the proof line is the signature texture. Flip the controls above to compare directions and modes — your pick becomes the system every screen inherits. (Shift+D toggles dark.)

Foundations

Color is meaning, never decoration

Every color in the system encodes something learnable: status (the approval gate), the strength/score ramp, freshness, and pipeline stage. This restraint is what makes the meaningful colors legible.
